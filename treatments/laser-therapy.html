Frequently Asked Questions

The number of sessions varies depending on your specific condition and treatment goals. Most laser treatments require 3-6 sessions spaced 4-6 weeks apart for optimal results.

Most patients describe the sensation as a slight rubber band snap against the skin. We use advanced cooling systems and may apply topical anesthetics to ensure your comfort during the procedure.

Downtime varies by treatment type. Non-ablative lasers typically have no downtime, while fractional treatments may require 2-3 days of redness. Your dermatologist will provide specific aftercare instructions.
