Frequently Asked Questions

Most patients need 1-3 sessions depending on their goals and the treatment area. CoolSculpting typically shows results after 1-2 sessions, while Kybella for double chin may require 2-4 treatments. Your provider will create a personalized plan during your consultation.

Most patients experience only mild discomfort. CoolSculpting may cause a cold sensation initially followed by numbness. Kybella injections feel like a series of small pinches. We use techniques to maximize your comfort throughout the procedure.

Results gradually appear as your body naturally processes and eliminates the destroyed fat cells. Most patients begin to see changes in 3-4 weeks, with optimal results visible after 2-3 months. The body continues to flush out fat cells for up to 6 months after treatment.

Yes, the fat cells eliminated through these treatments are permanently gone. However, maintaining a stable weight is important as remaining fat cells can still expand with weight gain. We recommend a healthy lifestyle to maintain your results long-term.
