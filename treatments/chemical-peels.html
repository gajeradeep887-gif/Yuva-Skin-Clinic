Skin Renewal Through Chemical Exfoliation

Our medical-grade chemical peels safely remove damaged outer layers of skin to reveal smoother, more youthful-looking skin beneath. We customize peel strength and ingredients to target your specific concerns.

Treatment Benefits:

  • Improves skin texture and tone
  • Reduces fine lines and wrinkles

Frequently Asked Questions

During your consultation, our dermatologist will assess your skin type, concerns, and goals to recommend the most appropriate peel. Factors like skin sensitivity, desired results, and available downtime all help determine the ideal treatment.

Downtime varies by peel depth. Superficial peels may have no downtime, medium peels typically involve 3-7 days of peeling, while deep peels require 1-2 weeks of recovery. We'll provide detailed aftercare instructions specific to your treatment.

For optimal results, we typically recommend a series of 3-6 treatments spaced 2-4 weeks apart for superficial peels, or 1-3 treatments spaced several months apart for deeper peels. Maintenance treatments are recommended every 3-6 months.
