Comprehensive Skin Cancer Detection

Early detection is crucial for successful skin cancer treatment. Our dermatologists use advanced screening technology and years of expertise to identify suspicious lesions at their earliest stages.

Screening Benefits:

  • Early detection of melanoma and other skin cancers
  • Peace of mind about suspicious moles
  • Digital mole mapping for tracking changes
  • Expert evaluation by skin cancer specialists
  • Quick and painless examination process

Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend annual screenings for everyone, especially those with: fair skin, family history of skin cancer, numerous moles, history of sunburns, or immunosuppression. People with higher risk factors may need more frequent screenings.

Our dermatologist will examine your entire body, including scalp, between toes, and other hard-to-see areas. Suspicious lesions may be examined with a dermatoscope for magnified viewing. If needed, a quick biopsy may be performed.

We recommend monthly self-examinations. Look for the ABCDEs of melanoma: Asymmetry, Border irregularity, Color variation, Diameter larger than 6mm, and Evolving size/shape/color. Any concerning changes should prompt a professional evaluation.
