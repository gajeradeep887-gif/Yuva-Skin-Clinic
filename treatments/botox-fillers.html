Frequently Asked Questions

Botox typically lasts 3-4 months. Hyaluronic acid fillers generally last 6-18 months depending on the product and treatment area. Sculptra provides results that can last up to 2 years. Individual results may vary based on metabolism and lifestyle factors.

Discomfort is minimal. We use ultra-fine needles and may apply topical numbing cream before treatment. Most fillers also contain lidocaine to enhance comfort during the procedure. Patients typically describe the sensation as a slight pinch or pressure.

Box results become noticeable in 3-7 days, with full effects visible at 2 weeks. Fillers provide immediate results, though slight swelling may make the initial result appear more pronounced. Final results are visible after any swelling subsides (typically 1-2 weeks).
